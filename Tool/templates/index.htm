Student Internet Governance Forum

Internet and Technology Policy for the Youth

About Us

SIGF is a platform for young adults to present their views on core internet policy and technology, as well as on the various intersections of it with other policy fields, like finance, health, and education. It acts a bridge between the stakeholders in the field of internet, technology, and digital policy and the youth.

Conference

The Student's Internet Governance Forum (SIGF) is planning to hold a Conference on the 1st of October, 2022 in a multistakeholderism manner, wherein high school students will present their views and research, and debate on pressing issues in the field of internet and digital policy. The debates will permit the students to develop a deeper understanding of the aforementioned issues through a highly engaging and interactive format.
