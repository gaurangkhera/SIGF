SIGF Challenge 2

Guidelines

To ensure equitable access to the SIGFChallenge, the challenge will operate dual-route. The first method of participation is via a sixty-second video, which is to be uploaded to a social media handle. The other method of participation is via an approximately 100 word written response. Participation via the first method is highly encouraged.

The best responses will be featured on our social media pages and in our events. The creators/authors of the best responses will also receive a certificate of merit, and an opportunity to participate in SIGF events and interface with policymakers and researchers.

Route 1: Video

Create a 60 second video on any topic related to the internet, technology, social media, or digital society that affects you as a student. Upload the same to Instagram and tag @studentigf, and also add the following hashtag in your caption for the post/reel: #sigfchallenge Mention your full name, school, and grade/standard/year in the caption.

Route 2: Written Response

Write a 100 word piece on any topic related to the internet, technology, social media, or digital society that affects you as a student.
